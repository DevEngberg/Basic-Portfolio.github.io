About Me

Greetings, Folks!

My name is Chris, and I'm a passionate designer located in NE Minneapolis. I have a black belt in cheese consumption and I'm a design nerd! I have over a decade of experience designing for small and large companies. This includes in-house experience and freelance work.

Working with other creative people is very important to me and I would love to work with you! I specialize in digital/print design, branding, illustration, and web design. Contact me for your next project!

Clients I've Worked With: American Heart Association, Universal Studios, Mark Mallman, Allegra Print & Imaging, Design Press.

I'm married, have the coolest border terrier dog named Arthur, and expecting my first child this spring. It's a boy!
